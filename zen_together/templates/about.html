How this works:


Welcome to ZenTogether, a game designed to help you do absolutely nothing. For everyone who needs a moment of zen, we encourage you to use ZenTogether to track your meditation progress. As you meditate with friends, your karma points increase according to the number of friends meditating together. Two mindful friends each receive double points, three friends triple points, etc.
